# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience.

## Our Standards

- Welcoming and inclusive language
- Respectful of differing viewpoints
- Graceful acceptance of constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to project maintainers.
